⎯ The problem

Nursing homes deserve better technology

Today you manage patients in Excel, track coverage in a notebook, and remember bed assignments from memory. Existing systems are built for large hospitals.

📋

Manual processes

Admissions, beds, and insurance coverage tracked on Excel or paper. High error rates, no audit trail, no traceability. Hours wasted on tasks that should take minutes.

🏗️

Oversized software

Existing systems are designed for 500+ bed hospitals. Expensive (USD 50K–200K), complex to deploy, and packed with features you'll never use.

🔗

No payer connectivity

Billing to insurance providers, coverage verification, and collections tracking are disconnected from daily operations. You lose revenue on uncollected claims.

⎯ MVP Modules

Three modules, one system

Everything you need to operate your nursing home or clinic from Day 1. Fully functional, no complex implementation.

👤

Residents / Patients

Manage each resident with a complete profile and an onboarding process that takes less than 5 minutes.

  • Simplified 3-step admission
  • Full profile with 7 tabs
  • Statuses: Active · Absent · Discharged · Deceased
  • Completeness indicator (0–100%)
  • Full history of movements
🏛️

Payers / Insurance Providers

Master catalog of insurance providers with plans, coverage, and agreements. All connected to your residents.

  • Plan and coverage management
  • Agreements with validity dates and rates
  • Expiration alerts at 30 days
  • Unique tax ID validation
  • Accidental deletion protection
🛏️

Beds & Occupancy

Visual real-time occupancy dashboard. Always know exactly how many beds are available.

  • Hierarchy: Building → Floor → Room → Bed
  • Real-time occupancy dashboard
  • States: Free · Occupied · Cleaning · Maintenance
  • Assignment with full audit trail
  • 30-second refresh rate
