Why now

The sector can no longer absorb fragmentation

Four converging forces make 2026 the inflection point for healthcare digitalization in Argentina.

01 — Regulation

Ley de Salud Digital

Argentina's digital health law is accelerating the push toward electronic records and digitalized administration across all health institutions.

02 — Demographics

Aging population

Rising demand from an aging population is increasing healthcare utilization and operational load at exactly the moment when capacity is strained.

03 — System complexity

Fragmentation costs

Clinics already manage dozens of disconnected tools and insurer integrations. Coordination is becoming unsustainable without a unified layer.

04 — Market gap

No solution fits

Enterprise tools are too heavy. Paper and Excel are too fragile. The 70% of providers with under 100 beds have no purpose-built option.
